How to Prepare

Master your assessment with these scientifically-backed preparation strategies.

Phase 1: Aptitude & Logic

This round tests your fluid intelligence and problem-solving speed. Here is how to excel:

🧩 Logical Patterns

Practice identifying shapes, rotations, and sequence changes. Focus on analogies and syllogisms.

🔢 Numerical Speed

Brush up on basic arithmetic, percentages, and data interpretation. The key is accuracy under pressure.

🧘 Mental Readyness

Ensure you are in a quiet environment.Fluid intelligence is highly sensitive to fatigue and distractions.

Phase 2: Technical Coding

We evaluate not just if your code works, but HOW it works. Focus on these areas:

✨ Clean Code Architecture

Use descriptive variable names and follow PEP 8 standards. AI evaluation rewards maintainability.

📊 Complexity Analysis

Be ready to explain the Big O time and space complexity of your solution. Efficiency is paramount.

🛠️ Edge Case Handling

Consider empty inputs, large datasets, and invalid formats. Robustness is a key metric.

Top 3 Preparation Tips

  • Mock Tests: Take at least 2 practice assessments to get used to the fullscreen and anti-cheat environment.
  • Review AI Solutions: After every test, carefully study the AI-generated solutions to understand more efficient methods.
  • Time Management: Don't get stuck on one question. Mark it and move on; fluid intelligence works best when you keep the momentum.
